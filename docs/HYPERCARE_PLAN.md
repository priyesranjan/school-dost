# Hypercare Plan (Post Go-Live)

## Window
- Day 0 to Day 14 after launch.

## Monitoring
- Track login failures, OTP send/verify failures, upload failures.
- Track notice publish delays and audit integrity failures.
- Track user-reported blockers by module.

## SLA Targets
- Critical issue response: 30 minutes.
- High issue response: 4 hours.
- Medium issue response: 1 business day.

## Daily Routine
- Review incident queue.
- Validate audit integrity status.
- Verify backup snapshot export availability.
- Publish summary to project stakeholders.

## Exit Criteria
- No open critical issues for 5 consecutive business days.
- P95 key workflows (login, notice publish, fee ops) stable.
- Operations team sign-off.
